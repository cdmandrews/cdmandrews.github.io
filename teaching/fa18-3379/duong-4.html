The Canoeist's "Grub Box"

  • Box Material
    1. Wood
    2. Tin
    3. Galvanized iron
  • Features
    1. Water-tight
    2. Varnished with shellac or boat varnish inside and out
    3. Made up of 5/16 pine if material is wood
    4. Top and bottom pieces heavier about 3/8 of an inch if used as seat
    5. Joining and dove-tailing must be done

Water-tight Tins and Waterproof Bags for Surplus Provisions

  • Spices

  • tea and/or cocoa
  • Sugar, Salt, Pepper
  • Flour and/or meal
  • Rice

    1. Store spices in small spice boxes with two covers
    2. Carry in flour/coffee/rice
