Clouds for everyone

On this page are all of the forty word clouds we created from the five most recent issues of selected scholarly journals in digital rhetoric and humanities, rhetoric and technology, and computers and writing (using the TagCrowd word frequency visualization tool).

We invite you to browse these clouds and consider what they reveal or obscure about familiar publications, about rhetoric, about digital rhetoric, and even about word clouds themselves.
