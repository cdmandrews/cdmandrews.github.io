The classroom: Clouds in practice

Each member of the class created word clouds of a different academic journal. Click on a class member's sprite to view their word clouds and commentary.

Taking on our role as word watchers, the graduate students in this course were invited to make define, reflect on, and make connections between words in publications related to digital rhetoric. Then, in trying to understand those words in their uses, the class came together to share and discuss their findings across different journals.

Prompted by their word clouds, we reflected on a diverse set of themes, including QA: LINK THESE accessibility, fun versus usefulness, transitioning to the job market, Master's-level education, research and reading, methodology, creativity, digital rhetoric, theory and practice, and expectations...
