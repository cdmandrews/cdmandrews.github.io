The classroom: Clouds in practice

Taking on our role as word watchers, graduate students in this course were invited to make, reflect on, and make connections between tag clouds based on selected academic journals. Then, in trying to understand those words in their uses, we came together to share and discuss findings across different journals.

Click on a class member's sprite to view their word clouds and analytical commentary. Prompted by our word clouds, we reflected on a diverse set of themes and possibilites for word watching, including theory and practice, accessibility, fun versus usefulness, transitioning from education to the job market, research and reading, methodology, digital rhetoric, expectations, and the peculiar in-betweenness of Master's-level graduate education.
