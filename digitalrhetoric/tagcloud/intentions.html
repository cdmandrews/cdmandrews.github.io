The original intentions: they are three-fold

The course...

Ultimately, then, my goals for using word clouds in this graduate course were:

  1. As a practice of novitiate word-watching appropriate for an introductory graduate seminar in digital rhetoric in a more-or-less traditionally generalist MA in English, offering threshold concepts for studnets locating themselves in rleation to the field (if not entering it),
  2. As a way into digital rhetoric practice as an object(ive) for multimodal digital authoring
  3. As a way into digital rhetoric theory, allowing us to see rhetorical ecologies of practice at work and treat our multimodal authoring as a method of inquiry into theory.
